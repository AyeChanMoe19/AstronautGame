Game Manual

Welcome to the Astronaut Adventure! Here's how to navigate your journey:

Controls

Space: Jump
D: Double Jump
C: Crawl
F: Flight

Collect 80 stars to unlock the ability to fly. This feature will be available for only 3 seconds!

Level up by scoring 50 points each time, up to a maximum of 250 points.
